Post #1

Based on the article, I am considering including a progress bar in my question answering. Although I do not plan to have many questions, I still think communicating progress towards completion would motivate the users to continue on through the task. I am going with a multiple choice format, where each step is a single question. I will also include a clear “next” button so the user does not have to spend time looking for how to progress to the next step. I am inspired to do this because of a website I discovered last Valentine’s day called Love Pawsona.In this website, multiple choice questions are used to determine what animal represents someone’s way of loving. I think this format will translate well because in my madlib, someone will be creating a Vampire persona through filling out multiple choice questions.
