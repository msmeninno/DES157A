For this project, I want to create an interactive exploration of my room that will show my relationship with young womanhood. I want users to be able to explore the room in a couple different ways.

  1. Move around the room with the cursor
  2. Hovering over sections to see what is clickable
  3. Clicking on sections to see details. This will change the image on the screen or open an overlay of a close up
  4. Hover over items in sections, where dialogue will be provided showing the items reason being included in the project

In my initial sketches, I only have different layouts of my room. I need to continue to the imagery for the rest of my project. I am considering using actual images, instead of drawings, of the items I plan on including in my room. For example, I can photoshop select and image of my cat, and place him on my bed.

In terms of developement, I am currently working on getting the panning feature to work. To do so, I have consulted with Professor Mead. He has adviced me to use some example code provided in the course resources to help me write my own. I am planning by Thursday to have a semi-function panning feature and to have further developed my graphics.
